## 1. Introduction

FlexMatches LLC ("FlexMatches", "we", "us", or "our") operates the FlexMatches™ mobile application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using FlexMatches, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

- **Account information:** Email address, username, password (encrypted)
- **Profile information:** Full name, bio, city, fitness level, sports preferences, preferred workout times, training intent, gender (optional), weight (optional), certifications (optional)
- **Profile photo:** Avatar image you upload
- **Messages:** Content of messages you send to matched partners
- **Workout data:** Exercise type, duration, dates logged
- **Session data:** Scheduled workout sessions with partners (sport, date, time, location)
- **Community data:** Circles you create or join, events you attend
- **Feedback:** Bug reports, support requests, survey responses

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Device information:** Device type, operating system, app version
- **Usage data:** Screens visited, features used, time spent (when analytics are enabled)
- **Push notification tokens:** Expo push token for sending notifications to your device
- **Location data:** City-level location for matching nearby partners (only when you grant permission)

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

- **Apple Sign-In:** If you sign in with Apple, we receive your name and email (or a relay email if you choose to hide your address)
- **Google Places:** When you search for a city, the query is sent to Google Places API. We store only the selected city name, not your search history.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- **Provide the service:** Match you with compatible fitness partners based on sport, fitness level, schedule, and location
- **Enable communication:** Deliver messages between matched users in real-time
- **Facilitate sessions:** Schedule, track, and confirm workout sessions
- **Track progress:** Maintain streak counts, workout logs, and achievement badges
- **Send notifications:** Push notifications for match requests, messages, session updates, and streaks
- **Improve the App:** Analyze usage patterns to fix bugs and improve features (when analytics are enabled)
- **Enforce policies:** Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, harassment, and terms violations
- **Process payments:** Manage Pro subscriptions through Apple's In-App Purchase system

We do NOT:
- Sell your personal data to third parties
- Use your data for advertising
- Share your data with data brokers
- Use your workout data for purposes other than the App's features

## 4. How We Share Your Information

### 4.1 With Other Users

Your profile information (name, photo, city, sports, fitness level, bio) is visible to other FlexMatches users in the Discover, Search, and Circles features. Your messages are only visible to the matched user you are chatting with.

### 4.2 With Service Providers

- **Supabase:** Database hosting, authentication, and real-time services (servers in the US)
- **Expo:** Push notification delivery and app update distribution
- **Apple:** In-App Purchase processing (iOS)
- **Google:** Places API for city search

### 4.3 As Required by Law

We may disclose your information if required by law, subpoena, court order, or government request, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of FlexMatches, our users, or the public.

## 5. Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored on Supabase (PostgreSQL) with Row Level Security (RLS) enabled
- Passwords are hashed — we never store plaintext passwords
- All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
- Sensitive operations (account deletion, admin actions) use server-side Edge Functions with JWT verification
- Push tokens are cleared from our database when you log out

While we implement industry-standard security measures, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 6. Data Retention

- **Active accounts:** We retain your data as long as your account is active
- **Deleted accounts:** When you delete your account (Settings > Danger Zone > Delete Account), we permanently remove all your data including: profile, matches, messages, workouts, sessions, notifications, push tokens, and your authentication record. This action cannot be undone.
- **Inactive accounts:** We may delete accounts that have been inactive for 24+ months after sending a notification email

## 7. Your Rights

### 7.1 Access and Portability
You can view all your personal data in the App's Profile and Settings screens.

### 7.2 Correction
You can update your profile information at any time through the Profile edit mode.

### 7.3 Deletion
You can permanently delete your account and all associated data through Settings > Danger Zone > Delete Account. This uses a secure server-side process that removes your data from all systems.

### 7.4 Opt-Out of Notifications
You can disable push notifications in Settings > Notifications, or through your device's system settings.

### 7.5 Location Data
Location permission is optional. You can deny or revoke location access in your device settings. The App will still work but cannot show distance-based partner recommendations.

### 7.6 California Residents (CCPA)
If you are a California resident, you have the right to: know what personal information we collect, request deletion of your data, and opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@flexmatches.com.

### 7.7 European Residents (GDPR)
If you are in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights including: the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, and data portability. Our legal basis for processing is consent (account creation) and legitimate interest (service operation). To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@flexmatches.com.

## 8. Children's Privacy

FlexMatches is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has created an account, we will delete it immediately. If you believe a child under 13 is using FlexMatches, please contact us at privacy@flexmatches.com.

## 9. Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services (such as gym websites). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the App or by email. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy indicates when the latest revision was made. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
